Highlights

Outside of class, I’ve built my leadership and execution style through athletics and community work. Being a student athlete taught me consistency, fast feedback loops, and how to perform under pressure without losing the team.

I’m also drawn to environments where inclusion is treated as a real input to better decisions. I care about who gets considered in the process, how messaging lands with different audiences, and whether the final experience actually works for more people.

In practice, that means I’m the person who can take a messy brief, ask the right questions, structure the problem, and deliver something people can use.

Core strengths

  • Ownership with momentum: I move work forward while keeping stakeholders aligned.
  • Clarity and persuasion: I write and present in a way that makes decisions easier.
  • Inclusive thinking: I consider different user contexts and edge cases early.
  • Iterative execution: I refine quickly through feedback and lightweight testing.

What I’m looking for

I’m drawn to product management and product marketing roles where I can help shape both the what and the why behind a product. I enjoy supporting thoughtful decision-making, refining messaging, and contributing to experiences that feel intentional, accessible, and genuinely useful to the people they serve.
