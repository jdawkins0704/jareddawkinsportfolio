Overview

College Chaos is a card game built around the stuff that makes campus life feel like its own universe. The goal was to create something fast, funny, and instantly playable, without needing a long rulebook or a complicated setup.

My Role

  • Defined the concept, game vibe, and content categories
  • Wrote prompt and response cards that are specific, relatable, and fun to play in groups
  • Designed the visual card system so the game feels consistent across every category
  • Created mockups that show what the game looks like mid-round

How I approached it

I treated this like a real product build, not just a creative idea. I started by setting constraints. The cards had to be readable in one glance, the tone had to feel bold without being forced, and the categories needed to cover different sides of college life so the game stays fresh. From there, I built a visual system that could scale as the deck grows.

Outcome

  • Ended with a cohesive card design and content system that can scale to a full deck
  • Built a visual direction that feels recognizable, playful, and on brand
  • Practiced turning an idea into a tangible, testable experience from start to finish
