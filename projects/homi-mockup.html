Overview

Homi is a prototype style dashboard for college roommates. The idea is simple. Most roommate conflict comes from unclear expectations and small things that pile up. Homi turns those everyday friction points into lightweight systems, so the house runs smoother and people feel heard.

My Role

  • Helped shape the concept into a clear set of features that solve real roommate problems
  • Designed a structured dashboard experience that feels easy to scan and easy to use
  • Built and presented the mockup as part of a team deliverable
  • Helped create a story that made the product feel real, not just theoretical

Result

This project helped my group land first place in our presentations because it felt practical and complete. It was not just a concept. It looked and behaved like something you could actually use, which made the value easy to understand.
