Marvel Explorer Guide

An interactive web experience designed to make exploring the Marvel universe intuitive, structured, and enjoyable.

Back to projects View live project

Overview

The Marvel Explorer Guide is a web-based experience designed to help users navigate the Marvel universe in a way that feels clear rather than overwhelming. The goal was to take a dense collection of characters and stories and make them approachable through structure and thoughtful design.

My role

  • Designed the overall user experience and navigation flow
  • Built the interface using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Focused on hierarchy, spacing, and clarity
  • Iterated based on usability and readability

Process & thinking

I approached this project as a product problem rather than a purely technical one. My goal was to reduce friction and make exploration feel natural, even for users unfamiliar with the Marvel universe.

By prioritizing structure and intentional layout decisions, the experience encourages exploration without overwhelming the user.

Outcome

  • A clean, intuitive interface that supports discovery
  • A responsive layout that works across devices
  • A clearer understanding of information architecture and UX thinking
