Overview

While supporting Case Construction (CNH Industrial), I created an in-depth Sales Walkaround Guide focused on backhoe loader attachments. The purpose was to give dealers a single resource they could use to quickly understand each attachment, explain it clearly, and highlight value in real customer conversations.

My Role

  • Researched and synthesized information across 20+ construction attachments
  • Turned technical specs into benefit-focused, dealer-friendly language
  • Organized content for quick scanning during walkarounds
  • Used consistent structure so each attachment section is easy to follow

How I approached it

Dealers don’t just need product facts. They need a clear way to connect features to real-world use cases. I focused on making the guide practical in the moment: what it is, what it does, who it’s for, and how to position it confidently without overloading the buyer.

Outcome

  • Delivered a structured, dealer-ready resource that supports sales effectiveness
  • Improved clarity and consistency across attachment explanations
  • Strengthened my ability to translate complex product info into usable storytelling
