About

My work is values led, but it is also built to perform.

I’m a junior student athlete at Washington University in St. Louis studying Marketing and Organization and Strategic Management, with a minor in Human Computer Interaction. I care about building experiences that are inclusive, clear, and genuinely useful.

My why

My interest in product and marketing comes from one core belief. The choices we make in design and storytelling shape who feels included and who gets left out.

I want a career where I can combine strategy, creativity, and structured thinking to build work that actually opens doors for more people. I care deeply about access and representation, not as a buzzword, but because inclusive thinking leads to stronger ideas, better decisions, and more meaningful impact.

What matters to me

Building with intent. Communicating with clarity. Making sure more people are considered in the process.

How I work

  • I start with the user and the real constraint.
  • I organize the problem into decisions and tradeoffs.
  • I ship something clear, then iterate based on feedback.

At a glance

Focus Product and marketing roles
Edge Inclusive thinking as structure
Style Bold ideas, clean execution

What I am building toward

Roles where I can support product decisions, shape messaging, and contribute to experiences that feel accessible and high quality.
